How to play El Pollo Loco

You can control Pepe with your arrow-keys on your keyboard.

While walking through the dessert of Mexico, you can pick up Coins and Salsabottles.

Attention: The chickens of Mexico do not like you! If they touch you, you will get hurt.

To avoid any damage, you can jump over the small chickens using SPACE-key.

Please note, Any key is not available. Pun intended...

If you jump on them, please enjoy your meal.

At the end of your journey, El Pollo Loco awaits you. To defeat her, you can use your bottles.

Press "D" to throw bottles at her. But be careful, you do not have infinite amounts of bottles.

Western theme music by 'Flower Mantis'.
